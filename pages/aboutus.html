Welcome, Student

Our Mission

The College of Computer Studies (CCS) is dedicated to providing a high-quality technical education that blends theoretical foundations with hands-on innovation. In line with our motto—Inceptum, Innovatio, Muneris (Beginning, Innovation, Service)—we strive to produce globally competitive IT professionals.

What We Do

The CCS Sit-in Monitoring System is designed to help students track their lab sessions, manage their time effectively, and make the most out of their available sit-in hours. This system allows students to:

  • Reserve lab sessions in advance
  • Track their remaining sit-in hours
  • View their sit-in history
  • Stay updated with announcements

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in computing education that empowers students to become innovative leaders in technology.
