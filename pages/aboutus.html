Our Mission

The College of Computer Studies (CCS) is dedicated to providing a high-quality technical education that blends theoretical foundations with hands-on innovation. In line with our motto—Inceptum, Innovatio, Muneris (Beginning, Innovation, Service)—we strive to produce globally competitive IT professionals.

The Monitoring System

This Sit-in Monitoring System was developed to streamline the laboratory experience for both students and faculty. Our goal is to ensure:

  • Efficiency: Real-time tracking of laboratory hours and availability.
  • Accountability: Accurate logging of student "sit-in" sessions for extra practice and research.
  • Accessibility: Easy access to lab resources to foster a culture of continuous learning.

Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1983, the College of Computer Studies has been a cornerstone of technological advancement at the University of Cebu. We continue to evolve alongside the industry, ensuring our students are equipped with the skills needed for the digital age.
